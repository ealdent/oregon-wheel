Color, type, and the brand logic of AI tools.

A researched catalog of 22 popular AI apps and agentic tools, mapping their light/dark palettes, typography choices, and the design thesis each system appears to be communicating.

Reading key

What counts as evidence?

Official means a brand or design-system source published colors, type, or design rationale. Mixed combines official assets with reputable brand indexes or direct product observation. Inferred means public standards were limited, so the palette/type is a close visual read, not a claim of canonical tokens.
