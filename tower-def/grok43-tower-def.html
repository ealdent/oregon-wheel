How to Play

1. Place Towers — Click a tower in the shop, then click on the map to place it (away from the glowing path).

2. Defend the Path — Enemies follow the dream path. Stop them before they reach the end!

3. Upgrade Between Waves — After each wave, click any tower to upgrade it (up to 4 upgrades).

4. Earn Gold — Defeat enemies to buy more towers and upgrades.

5. Survive 20 Waves — Different enemies have unique speeds and strengths. Good luck, Dream Guardian!
